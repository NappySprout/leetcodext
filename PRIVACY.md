# Privacy Policy

**Code Cultivator** does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Storage

The extension stores your progress (question queue, completion pointer, cycle count, and settings) using `chrome.storage.sync`. This data is stored locally on your device and, if you are signed into a Chrome profile, synced across your devices via Google's infrastructure. This sync is handled entirely by Google — the extension has no server of its own and never sees or transmits your data.

## Third-Party Services

No analytics, tracking, or third-party SDKs are used.

## Contact

If you have any questions, please open an issue on the project repository.
